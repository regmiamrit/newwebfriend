About Webfriend

Web friend is a web design and development agency that focus on creating well-designed and functional websites at a reasonable price.

We are a team that creates professional and affordable websites for small businesses. We understand the digital needs for a new business who are seeking a larger audience on the web and are here to help with the process from start to finish. We offer easy all-inclusive options for any company to get started and attract more customers to their business. Contact us and we can discuss what you may require.

We are co-workers at a small company that has a focus in the education and recruitment area. During our 7+ years there we have worked closely with the directors and marketing team to learn the inside and out of what a business needs to strive on the internet. We are huge fans of simple and effective web designs and is what we hope to continue to create and work on. We love working with small businesses and helping them achieve their goals is what we do.
